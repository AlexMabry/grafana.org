Community

Grafana is an open-source project which relies heavily on community involvement. You can get involved in many ways for example: provide feedback, contribute features or bugfixes, improve to the documentation, answer user questions and help Grafana grow.

Events & Meetups


November 30 - December 1, 2016 | 9:00am EST - New York City, NY

GrafanaCon 2016


Democratizing Metrics in New York City.

Join the Grafana team, along with hundreds of monitoring and data aficionados, for our second annual GrafanaCon. This two-day event will feature content and speakers from across the Grafana ecosystem, led by Torkel Odegaard, the creator of Grafana.

Day 1 will be several talks and panels of general interest, in single track format. Day 2 is all about rolling your sleeves up: a multi-track format with deeper dives, along with a Grafana Plugin lounge.

If you are a Grafana code contributor and we have not already contacted you, please contact us for free or discounted travel arrangements.

Refreshments and Lunch will be provided.
